Delivering Great Local Schools

From Alphington to Ararat and everywhere in between, we've been investing in our schools - and our students.

Across our state, the Andrews Labor Government is building 70 new schools and fixing 1,300 more.

At the same time, our Breakfast Clubs are making sure kids gets the most important meal of the day, while our support for uniforms and shoes, camps and excursions is helping families cover these essential costs.

But we know there's more to do.

It’s why a re-elected Andrews Labor Government will continue to build and upgrade schools across Victoria, making sure that wherever they live, every child gets the great education they deserve.
